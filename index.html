Rock Paper Scissors

Mario's Princess is in Danger again. But since he's to old to save her again, he asked you to do it for him.

And since the dragon also isn't in a good shape anymore, he prefers do decide her fate with the simple game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.

You are playing against the dragon which captured her. Whichever gets to 5 points will keep the princess.
